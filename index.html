About Us

Who We Are

Africa Water Ambassadors (AWA), formerly known as Kenya Water Ambassadors (KWA), was established in 2023 following the Ewaso Nyiro Journey of Water Campaign-an initiative spearheaded by WWF-Kenya and other partners.Africa Water Ambassadors(AWA) is a dynamic network of passionate young people,united in our commitment to create lasting change in the water sector. Our members are passionate young people driving concret action on water conservation, governance and sustainable management. We amplify the voices of young people, prioritizing water issues that matter most to us. Through collaboratin and collective action, we create spaces where youth expertise is recognised and valued. Above all,we raise awareness about the vital role water plays in building climate resilience and a sustainable future. Our future is our responsibility.

Mission

To provide a platform for multi-stakeholder engagement driven by policies and actions promoting responsible water resource management and sustainable practices.

Vision

A network of passionate young people dedicated to promoting water conservation and advocacy for healthy water ecosystems.

FAQs

What is the mission of Africa Water Ambassadors?

Africa Water Ambassadors (AWA) is a youth-led platform dedicated to raising awareness about the importance of conserving water resources. Our mission is to address water scarcity and global water challenges through sustainable water management practices.

Who can join Africa Water Ambassadors?

AWA is open to young professionals, water advocates, and students from across Kenya who are committed to protecting and conserving water resources.

How can I become a member of Africa Water Ambassadors?

You can become a member by filling out the membership form available on our website and contributing a membership fee of 1,000 KSH to support the official registration of AWA as a Non-Governmental Organization.

What are the benefits of joining AWA?

Registered members gain access to networking events, capacity-building activities, and opportunities to participate in both national and global water initiatives.

How can I support AWA's initiatives?

You can support AWA by donating, volunteering, or collaborating with us. Secure payment options for donations will be available soon.
